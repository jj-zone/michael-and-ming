About Michael and Ming

Meet Michael and Ming, two spirited brothers who went from shelter cats to ruling the roost in their forever home! These lively kitties have captured everyone's hearts and are now seizing the spotlight on YouTube. Whether they're tearing through the house on a wild chase, curled up for a cozy cuddle session, or unleashing chaos with their favorite toys, these two are guaranteed to keep you on the edge of your seat and leave you grinning from ear to ear.

Michael is physically stronger but shy and afraid of a lot of things, while Ming is smaller in stature but calm, quick, and cool most of the time and isn't afraid of much. Together, they create the perfect dynamic duo that will entertain and warm your heart!
