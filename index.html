About Michael and Ming

From the shelter to the spotlight—Michael and Ming, the dynamic duo of cats, are taking center stage. One's a cautious powerhouse with a heart of gold. The other's a fearless mastermind with lightning-fast reflexes. Together, they turn everyday life into a blockbuster of curiosity, comedy, and chaos.

Whether they're racing through the living room like action heroes, sharing a brotherly nap in the calm after the storm, or plotting their next grand escapade, Michael and Ming prove that the most unforgettable adventures start with nine lives and one unbreakable bond.
